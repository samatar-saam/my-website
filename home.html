AIMS

To protect and guide youth by raising awareness about addiction, risky behaviors, and the dangers of tahriib (irregular migration).

WHAT WE DO

We run workshops, mentorship programs, community outreach activities, and digital campaigns.

OUR MISSION

To reduce youth vulnerability by offering knowledge, positive role models, and opportunities for growth.

ADDICTION SUPPORT

We offer guidance, awareness sessions, and connection to rehabilitation services.

SAFE MIGRATION EDUCATION

We teach youth about the dangers of irregular migration and offer safe, legal alternatives.

SKILLS TRAINING

We help youth build practical skills like digital literacy and entrepreneurship.
